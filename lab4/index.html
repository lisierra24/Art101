Task 1: How to put your hair up in bun:

  1. First you need a hair tie and comb
  2. Comb any hair knots until hair is tangled and knot free
  3. Depending on the hair length and height preference place the ponytail base
  4. Do not let go of the base of the ponytail
  5. From the top of the ponytail start twisting to the bottom
  6. Wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail
  7. Finally, tie the bun with the hair tie
  8. You have a bun!
